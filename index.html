About me:

I’m a 3D artist and designer from Bologna, born in 1999. My interest in digital media began in high school through Java, Scratch, and an independent exploration of the Adobe Creative Suite, which first sparked my curiosity for design. During my first year of Architecture at Politecnico di Torino, a drawing course introduced me to 3ds Max with V-Ray, opening my eyes to the world of 3D and digital visualization. Since then, I have developed a largely self-taught practice focused on visual representation, interactive media, and a broad range of 3D tools. This path led to a collaboration with Primula Costruzioni, first through a curricular internship and later through an extracurricular internship, working on immersive real estate experiences.

Work 01

Immersive Real Estate Experience for Primula Costruzioni

Real-time architectural visualization software developed in Unreal Engine 5 to support the sales and presentation of a new residential building within its surrounding context. The project allows interactive exploration of the architecture and its interior spaces using real-time rendering and dynamic natural lighting, and is designed to be operated through a touch screen interface.

Work 02

Immersive Property Cards for Primula Costruzioni

A series of immersive property cards created to support the presentation and sale of renovated apartments within an existing building. A dedicated interactive page was developed for each unit, focusing on the interior spaces and communicating the character of every apartment through a clear and engaging experience. The website was built with Three.js and published via GitHub Pages. You can visit the page at: https://ilarioplacanica.github.io/Alfieri/A14/

Work 03

"CAPO" from "Necroville" - 2D to Real-Time 3D

A 3D adaptation of “Capo” from the 2D series Necroville, developed through a game and animation-oriented workflow. The character was modeled in Blender with clean quad-based topology designed for proper deformation, stylized texturing, and shader development. The project also includes lighting, a minimal environment setup, and pose and animation tests to explore how the character translates from a flat illustration into a production-ready 3D asset.
